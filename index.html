Nikolaos Sarafianos
nikos.sarafianos@gmail.com

My friends call me Nikos, and I'm a research scientist at Facebook Reality Labs. My research interests lie on the union of Computer Vision, Deep Learning and Computer Graphics with applications on virtual humans. I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Houston advised by Prof. Ioannis Kakadiaris where I worked on 3D human pose estimation, visual attributes and text-to-image retrieval. In the past, I've interned at Facebook Reality Labs and Amazon, Alexa. I'm trying to be an advocate for promoting female involvement in Computer Science and have volunteered twice at the Grace Hopper Celebration for women in computing and as an instructor at Girls Who Code. If you would like to know more about opportunities at FRL I am one email away. We would love to have applications from a diverse group of people and research areas.
