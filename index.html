Nikolaos Sarafianos
nikos.sarafianos@gmail.com

My friends call me Nikos, and I'm a research scientist at Facebook Reality Labs (Oculus Research) where I'm working on 3D humans. I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Houston advised by Prof. Ioannis Kakadiaris. My research interests lie in the intersection of Deep Learning with Computer Vision with most of my current focus being on 3D humans, visual attributes, text-to-image retrieval, and generative models. In the past, I've interned at Facebook Reality Labs and Amazon, Alexa. I'm trying to be an advocate for promoting female involvement in Computer Science and have volunteered twice at the Grace Hopper Celebration for women in computing and as an instructor at Girls Who Code. If you would like to know more about opportunities at FRL please do not hesitate to contact me. We would love to have applications from a diverse group of people and research areas.
