My friends call me Nikos, and I'm a fourth year Ph.D. student at CBL at the University of Houston advised by Prof. Ioannis Kakadiaris. My research interests lie in the intersection of Machine (and Deep) Learning with Computer Vision. Last summer I interned at Amazon, Alexa and worked on Deep Multi-Task Learning.
